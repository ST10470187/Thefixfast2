History

Fixfast crew a company founded in 2018 by three childhood best friends, What all started as a weekend hustle for Katlego, Adonai, and Thabo has a become a fully oiled machine providing affordable repairs and providing jobs to the youth

Mission

Their mission is to provide fast, affordable and quality home repairs providing a very necessary service for their community and they hope to eventually produce this service country wide
